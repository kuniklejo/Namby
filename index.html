Sami McGrath

Sami McGrath took a gap year after finishing high school to write songs & gig around the Sunshine Coast. After her year off, she called up Namby to help record + produce a release. Sami had 15 or so unarranged country-pop songs she'd written on guitar, so together they entered Squishy Studios III (the makeshift setup in the back room) to start arranging the songs and recording demos. Sami wrote the songs, sung the vocals and recorded the acoustic guitar. Namby programmed the drums, recorded the bass, electric guitar & grand piano, and mixed the tracks. Namby's background of rock, punk and alternative gelled with Sami's country-pop direction to make a pretty unique product. The spontaneity of the project ended up with three of the six songs which made the final cut being written during the actual recording process. Two months later, a collection of everything from ballads to alt rock jams emerged, in fitting with Namby's philosophy of zero autotune or pitch correct.
